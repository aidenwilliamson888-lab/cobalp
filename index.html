📖 Chapter 1: The Lost Kingdom

The wind carried the scent of ancient pines as Kaelen stepped onto the crumbling stone bridge. Below him, the river had long dried up, leaving only a whisper of what once was a mighty waterfall. Legend said that the last king of Eldoria had hidden his greatest treasure somewhere in these ruins.

Kaelen pulled out the old parchment his grandfather had given him. The ink was faded, but the map was still visible. "Three stones to the east, where the shadow of the eagle falls at dawn," he read aloud.

As he approached the marked spot, the ground beneath him trembled. A hidden door slid open, revealing a staircase that descended into darkness. Torches along the walls ignited one by one, lighting his path.

At the bottom of the stairs, a massive chamber awaited. In the center stood a pedestal with a glowing crystal. This was the Heart of Eldoria — the source of the kingdom's lost magic.
